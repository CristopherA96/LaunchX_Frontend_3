Preparation Before Vaccine

The success of vaccines is strongly influenced by the strength of the body's immune system. Therefore, there are several things that can be tried to make the COVID-19 vaccine work:

Avoid alcoholic beverages

Alcohol is a non-beneficial liquid for your body and by drinking alcohol could reduce the effect for the vaccine, it does not matter which one.

Avoid stress

Stress has a profound effect on inmmune work. Moreover, prolonged stress can increase cortisol production and oxidative stress in the body.

Eat healthy food

Eating healthy foods helps our body's defenses to be high. This makes it difficult for any virus to attack the body

Get enough sleep

Sleep has a positive impact on our health, causing the body to start a whole series of biochemical processes that make us perform physically and mentally when we get up

Exercise or physical activity

Regular exercise is an important part of a healthy lifestyle. People who lead active lives are less likely to get sick and more likely to live longer lives
